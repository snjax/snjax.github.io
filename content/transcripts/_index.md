Cleaned transcripts of my talks, panels, podcasts and interviews. Each is linked to its original video and published in two formats: a **human-readable page** and a **raw `.txt`** meant for LLMs and AI research agents to ingest.

Speech was transcribed with automatic speaker diarization, then edited to remove recognition artifacts and disfluencies; technical terms and names are normalized to match [the rest of this site](/bio/). For Russian-language sources, a Russian transcript and a separate English translation are both published.
